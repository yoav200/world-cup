Rules

You are more than welcome to join in, but before you do, please take few minutes to read the rules:

Your main goal is to gain as many points as possible
At the end of the tournament, top 3 users will win the pot
First place - 50% of the pot
Second place - 30% of the pot
Third place - 20% of the pot
pot size depends on how many users will join in
(our hosting costs will be taken from the pot (~50$))

And what if more than one user is in the first 3 places?
1. 2 users share the the highest score
They will split 80% (1st and 2nd prizes) of the pot between them
The second best score (1 or more users) will split the rest of the pot between them
The third best score will not win any prize in this case.
2. 3 or more users share the highest score - they split 100% of the pot between them
The second and third best doesn't win anything.
3. One user has the highest score, 2 or more users share the second highest score
The first place will take 50% of the pot
The users in the second place will split 50% (2nd and 3rd prizes) of the pot between them
Third best score doesn't win anything


Points calculation method:

Match Points (can be updated during the tournament, each match until it's kickoff time)
  • Correct match result (1X2) worth 1 point
  • Guessing exact match score worth another 2 points
  • (result is determined after 90 minutes, not including extra time/penalties)
e.g.:
Argentina-Iceland
You guessed Argentina wins 3-1
If Iceland wins or the match ends up with a draw - you get no points
If Argentina wins in every score except 3-1 - you get 1 point
If Argentina wins exactly 3-1 - you get 3 points

This is relevant to all tournament matches (from group stage till the final)

Bet on knockout matches can be done only once the match teams are known
Make sure you place your bet before knockout matches, you don't want to lose points because of that
Betting on knockout matches is done regardless to the teams chosen by you as qualifiers

Qualifier Points (can be changed until the tournament starts)
For every correct qualifier you get:
  • Round of 16 - 2 point
  • Quarter final - 4 points
  • Semi-final - 6 points
  • Final - 8 points
  • Third Place - 10 points
  • Winner - 12 points
