Rules

You are more than invited to join in, but before you do, please take few minutes to read the rules:

Your main goal is to gain as much points as possible
At the end of the tournament, top 3 users will win the pot
First place - 50% of the pot
Second place - 30% of the pot
Third place - 20% of the pot
pot size depends on how many users will join in
(our hosting costs will be taken from the pot (~50$))

Points calculation method:

Match Points (can be updated during the tournament, each match until it's kickoff time)
  • Correct match result (1X2) worth 1 point
  • Guessing exact match score worth another 2 points
  • (result is determined after 90 minutes, not including extra time/penalties)
e.g:
Argentina-Iceland
You guessed Argentina wins 3-1
If Iceland wins or the match ends up with a draw - you get no points
If Argentina wins in every score except 3-1 - you get 1 point
If Argentina wins exactly 3-1 - you get 3 points

This is relevant to all tournament matches (from group stage till the final)

Qualifier Points (can be changed until the tournament starts)
For every correct qualifier you get:
  • Round of 16 - 1 point
  • Quarter final - 2 points
  • Semi-final - 3 points
  • Final - 4 points
  • Guessing the correct Third place/Winner - 5 points
